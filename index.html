About Me

I'm Jonathan Zarger, an Electrical and Computer Engineering Master's student at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. I'm currently at an internship with Northrop Grumman Mission Systems. I have experience with hardware, software, control systems, and signal processing.

Interests
The focus of my Master's is Embedded Systems and Control Systems. I have strong interests in working with launch vehicles, satellites, or autonomous vehicles. I have fun tinkering with electronics, taking things apart to figure out how they work, and rapid prototyping of hardware and software.
